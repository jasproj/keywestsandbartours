Tiki boats are the most-requested party charter in Key West — custom-built floating bars with covered seating, sound systems, and a captain who knows every hidden sandbar in the backcountry. Pick a 2-hour sunset tiki cruise for couples, a full-day sandbar tiki trip for your crew, or a private bachelorette package with BYOB and Bluetooth playlist.

Pick Your Tiki Boat Experience

Sandbar tiki trip (full day, 6 hours). The flagship Key West tiki experience: cruise the backcountry, anchor at a knee-deep sandbar, float with a drink, swim, snorkel, and watch dolphins. Best for bachelorette weekends, birthdays, friend-group reunions, and any crew that wants the whole day on the water.

Sunset tiki cruise (2 hours). Tightly-timed evening charter past Mallory Square and into the Gulf to catch the sunset from a tiki-bar boat. Best for couples, anniversaries, low-key date nights, or a sunset add-on to a longer Key West weekend.

Bachelorette + private party tiki. Custom-curated private charters with extended hours, decorations, and playlist coordination. Operators like Salty Bottom and Keys Tikis run bachelorette-focused packages year-round; call us at (407) 476-6190 to match the right boat to your group size and vibe.

Group + private bookings. Tiki boats are private-charter by design — up to 12 passengers. Per-person rates are best with a full boat ($50–$80/person); smaller groups still get the whole boat to themselves. Tip your captain.

Frequently Asked Questions

A tiki boat is a custom floating tiki-bar pontoon — covered seating, built-in bar, sound system. Tours run as private 2–6 hour charters to the sandbar, sunset cruises, or party packages. Up to 12 passengers; captain included; BYOB.
They're one of the most popular bachelorette options in Key West. Private boat means your crew only, BYOB lets you control the bar, and the sandbar stop gives you shallow turquoise water for swimming, floating, and photos. Plan 2+ weeks ahead in peak season (April–June).
Sunset tiki tours (2 hours) start around $525 private. Full-day sandbar tiki trips (6 hours) typically $799–$949 for a private group up to 12. Per-person rates work out to $50–$80 with a full boat. Captain, fuel, and basic gear included.
Sunscreen (reef-safe preferred), towels, food, drinks (BYOB welcome on most tiki boats — confirm with your captain), Bluetooth playlist, swimsuits, waterproof phone case. Coolers, ice, and snorkel gear typically provided.
Most operators offer full refunds or rescheduling for weather cancellations. Your captain will contact you if conditions are unsafe. Light rain and moderate weather usually don't cancel a trip.
