FAQ

Common Questions

What sandbars will we visit?

Popular destinations include Snipes Point, Mud Keys, Woman's Key, Boca Chica, Jewfish Basin, and Boca Grande Key. Your captain will choose the best spot based on conditions, tide, and your preferences.

What should we bring?

Sunscreen (reef-safe please!), towels, sunglasses, and any food/drinks you want. Most charters provide coolers, ice, water, and snorkel gear. Cash for captain's tip is appreciated.

How many people can fit?

Boats range from 6-person private charters to 36-passenger party boats. We'll help you find the right size for your group.

What if weather is bad?

Most charters offer free cancellation or rescheduling if weather makes boating unsafe. Check individual listings for specific policies.
