Sandbar Questions

What's a sandbar?

A sandbar is a shallow area of sand that emerges from the ocean at low tide. Key West's backcountry is full of them ‚Äî knee-deep turquoise water, soft sand, and usually no one else around.

What should we bring?

Sunscreen (reef-safe preferred), towels, food & drinks (BYOB welcome), and a waterproof phone case. Most boats provide snorkel gear, coolers, and ice.

Will we see dolphins?

Very likely! Dolphins are common in the backcountry. Your captain knows where they hang out but can't guarantee sightings ‚Äî they're wild animals after all.

Is it good for kids?

Perfect for kids. Shallow water, soft sand, no waves. Bring their snorkel gear or use the boat's ‚Äî they'll see fish, starfish, and maybe rays.
