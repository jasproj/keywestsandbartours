Common Questions

What sandbars will we visit?

Popular spots include Snipes Point, Mud Keys, Woman Key, and Boca Grande Key. Your captain picks the best sandbar based on tides, weather, and crowd levels that day.

What should we bring?

Sunscreen (reef-safe please), towels, sunglasses, and any food/drinks you want. Most charters provide coolers, ice, water, and snorkel gear. Cash tip for captain appreciated.

How deep is the water at the sandbar?

Typically ankle to waist deep depending on tide. Perfect for wading, floating, and hanging out. Deeper water nearby for swimming and snorkeling.

What's the cancellation policy?

Most charters offer free cancellation 24-48 hours in advance. Weather cancellations are always refunded or rescheduled. Check individual listings for specific policies.
