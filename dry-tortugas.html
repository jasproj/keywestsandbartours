FAQ

Common Questions

Should I take the seaplane or ferry?

If budget allows, the seaplane is the better experience. You'll see the reef, shipwrecks, dolphins, and sharks from above ‚Äî views you simply can't get from the ferry. Plus, you save 4+ hours of travel time, giving you more time at the park or back in Key West.

Will I get seasick on the ferry?

The 2.5-hour ferry ride can be rough in choppy conditions. If you're prone to motion sickness, consider taking medication beforehand or opting for the seaplane, which offers a smooth, scenic flight.

What happens if weather cancels my trip?

Both operators offer full refunds for weather-related cancellations. The seaplane has a $50 change fee if you reschedule more than 48 hours out; no refunds within 48 hours for passenger cancellations.

Can I bring kids?

Yes! Both options are family-friendly. Children under 2 fly free on laps (seaplane). Kids 12 and under get discounted rates on both the seaplane and ferry.

How's the snorkeling?

World-class. Dry Tortugas has some of the clearest water and healthiest reefs in North America. No crowds, incredible visibility, and abundant marine life including sea turtles, rays, and tropical fish.

Ready to Visit Fort Jefferson?

Seats are limited and often sell out in advance.
