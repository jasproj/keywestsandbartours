Bachelorette FAQ

Can we bring decorations?

Yes! Bring banners, sashes, inflatables - whatever makes it special. Just no glitter (it's terrible for the ocean) and clean up before you go.

Is there a bathroom on the boat?

Most larger boats have a head (marine toilet). Smaller boats - you'll use the ocean or hold it. Ask when booking if this is important.

Can we play our own music?

Yes! Most boats have Bluetooth speakers. Bring a charged phone with your playlist ready. Captain controls the volume.

What about photographers?

Some operators can recommend local photographers who can join the trip. Or just designate your best friend with a waterproof phone case.

How many people can fit?

Boats range from 6 to 20+ guests. Tell them your group size and they'll recommend the right boat. Larger groups can sometimes book multiple boats.
