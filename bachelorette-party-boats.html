FAQ

Questions from Maids of Honor

How many girls can fit on one boat?

Most boats fit 6-12 comfortably. For larger crews (12-20+), we can help you find a bigger vessel or split into two boats that anchor together at the sandbar.

Can we bring champagne and drinks?

Yes! All boats are BYOB. Bring champagne, seltzers, whatever you want. Coolers and ice are provided. Pro tip: Freeze water bottles to keep drinks cold longer.

Do boats have Bluetooth speakers?

Most boats have Bluetooth speakers for your playlist. Check individual listings or ask when booking. Captains are used to bach party vibes.

What if the bride gets seasick?

The sandbars are in calm, protected waters ‚Äî not open ocean. Very few people get seasick. But if she's prone, take Dramamine 30 min before departure just in case.

Can we decorate the boat?

Light decorations are usually fine ‚Äî sashes, balloons, banners. Just ask the captain first and avoid anything that could damage the boat or blow away.
