FAQ

Luxury Charter Questions

What's included in a luxury charter?

Most include captain, fuel, safety equipment, and basic amenities. Premium vessels often include snorkel gear, paddleboards, floats, and water toys. Food/drinks are typically BYOB unless specified.

Can you help with proposals?

Absolutely. Many captains have helped stage proposals. Let them know in advance and they can help with timing, location, and even photography coordination.

What about bad weather?

All charters offer full refunds or rescheduling for weather cancellations. Your captain will contact you if conditions are unsafe.

How far in advance should I book?

For luxury vessels, 2-4 weeks ahead is recommended, especially during peak season (Dec-Apr). Last-minute availability sometimes exists.
