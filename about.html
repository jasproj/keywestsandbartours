About Sandbar Tours

Connecting travelers with the best local tour operators across the Florida Keys.

Our Mission

Key West Sandbar Tours was created with one simple goal: help visitors discover the best adventures the Florida Keys have to offer while supporting the local businesses that make this paradise so special.

We're not a tour company ourselves. Instead, we're a discovery platform that connects you directly with verified local tour operators. When you book through our site, you're booking directly with the people who know these waters best‚Äîthe captains, guides, and families who've called the Keys home for generations.

Why We Built This

Planning a trip to Key West can be overwhelming. There are dozens of tour companies, hundreds of options, and it's hard to know which operators are truly worth your time and money.

We've done the legwork for you. Every tour on our platform comes from a real, verified local operator. We partner with FareHarbor to ensure you get secure, instant booking with real-time availability.

No big travel agency markups. No outdated inventory. Just authentic Keys experiences from people who live and breathe island life.
