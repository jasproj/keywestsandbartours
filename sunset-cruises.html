Common Questions

What time should we book for sunset?

Check the sunset time for your date. Most cruises depart 1.5-2 hours before sunset to cruise, find the perfect spot, and settle in for the show.

Can we bring champagne?

Yes! Most charters are BYOB. Bring champagne, wine, beer, snacks - whatever makes your sunset special. Glass bottles are sometimes restricted, so check with your operator.

What if it's cloudy?

Cloudy sunsets are often the most spectacular - the clouds catch the color. Only severe weather triggers cancellations, which are always refunded.

Can you help with a proposal?

Absolutely. Let your captain know in advance. They'll help with timing, positioning for photos, and can even coordinate with photographers.
