FAQ

Sunset Questions

What time do sunset cruises leave?

Departure times vary by season to catch the actual sunset. Expect 5-7pm most of the year. Your operator will confirm exact times based on sunset that day.

Can you help with a proposal?

Absolutely! Many captains have helped stage proposals. Let them know in advance and they can help with timing, champagne, and even photography coordination.

What if it's cloudy?

Cloudy sunsets are often the most dramatic! If weather is truly bad, operators offer full refunds or rescheduling. No one wants you to miss a good one.

Is food/drinks included?

Varies by tour. Luxury charters often include champagne and appetizers. Others are BYOB. Check the listing or ask when booking.
