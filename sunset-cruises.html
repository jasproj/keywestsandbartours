What time do sunset cruises depart?

Most sunset cruises depart 1.5-2 hours before sunset to ensure you're in the perfect spot when the sun goes down. Exact times vary by season ‚Äî operators will confirm your departure time after booking.

Most private charters are BYOB friendly. You can bring your own food, drinks, and alcohol. Coolers and ice are typically provided. Some luxury cruises include drinks ‚Äî check each listing for specifics.

What if the weather is bad?

Captains monitor weather closely. If conditions are unsafe, you'll be offered a full refund or the option to reschedule. Light clouds can actually make for more dramatic sunset colors!
