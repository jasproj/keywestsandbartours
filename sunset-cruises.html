Sunset Questions

What time do sunset cruises leave?

Departure times vary by season. Expect 5-7pm most of the year. Your captain confirms exact time based on that day's sunset.

Can you help with a proposal?

Absolutely. Many captains have helped stage proposals. Let them know in advance ‚Äî they can help with timing, champagne, even photography coordination.

What if it's cloudy?

Cloudy sunsets are often the most dramatic. If weather is truly bad, operators offer full refunds or rescheduling.

Private vs shared ‚Äî what's the difference?

Private means just your group and captain. Shared cruises have other guests but cost less. Both see the same sunset.
