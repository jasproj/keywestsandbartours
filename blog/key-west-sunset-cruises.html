When to Book Your Sunset Cruise

Sunset times vary throughout the year, affecting cruise departure times:

  • Winter (Dec-Feb): Sunset around 6pm. Cruises depart 4:30-5pm. Cooler temps, bring jacket.
  • Spring (Mar-May): Sunset 7-8pm. Perfect weather, moderate crowds. Best overall season.
  • Summer (Jun-Aug): Sunset 8-8:30pm. Hot but beautiful. Watch for afternoon storms clearing.
  • Fall (Sep-Nov): Sunset 6:30-7:30pm. Fewer tourists, great deals. Hurricane season caution.

Frequently Asked Questions

Will I get seasick?
Most sunset cruises stay in calm, protected waters. Catamarans are especially stable. If prone to motion sickness, take Dramamine beforehand and choose a larger vessel.

Can I bring my own drinks?
Most cruises do not allow outside alcohol since drinks are included. Check your specific tour's policy.

What if it rains?
Operators monitor weather closely. If conditions are unsafe, you'll be rescheduled or refunded. Light rain sometimes clears for spectacular sunsets.

Are sunset cruises good for kids?
Many family-friendly options exist with mocktails for kids. Party boats and some adult-focused cruises may not be suitableâ€”check descriptions.
