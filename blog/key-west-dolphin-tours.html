Bottlenose dolphins call the warm waters around Key West home year-round. These intelligent, playful creatures frequently ride bow waves, leap alongside boats, and hunt in the shallow backcountry flats. Seeing dolphins in their natural habitat is a completely different experience than a marine park.

Key West offers multiple ways to encounter wild dolphins, from dedicated watching cruises to snorkel trips where dolphins make surprise appearances.

Best Season: Year-round in Key West. Spring and fall offer calmest seas.
