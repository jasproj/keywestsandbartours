Hurricane Season: Real Talk

Official hurricane season runs June 1 - November 30, with peak activity August-October. Should you avoid Key West during this time?

Not necessarily. Most days are beautiful. Hurricanes are well-forecasted days in advance. The tradeoff: significantly lower prices, virtually no crowds, and flexible booking policies.

Tips for hurricane season travel:

  • Book refundable accommodations
  • Get travel insurance
  • Monitor weather before departure
  • Have a flexible itinerary
