Frequently Asked Questions

Ferry or seaplane—which should I choose?
Ferry = budget & scenic boat ride. Seaplane = faster with aerial views. Both reach the same destination. Choose based on time and budget.
Do I need snorkel experience?
No! We teach beginners. Reef snorkeling is 20-30 feet deep. Our guides demo everything and stay close.
What time do we leave?
Ferry departs 7:15 AM, returns 5:15 PM. Seaplane departs 8:00 AM, returns 1:00 PM. Arrive 30 minutes early.
What if weather cancels the trip?
Rough seas may cancel. We'll reschedule free or refund fully. Monitor weather and confirm morning-of.
