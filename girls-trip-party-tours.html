Frequently Asked Questions

Can we bring our own music?
Yes! Connect via Bluetooth or aux cable. We'll play whatever your crew wants—songs, playlists, podcasts. Control it yourself.
Is BYOB allowed?
Absolutely. Bring beer, wine, liquor—whatever you want. We provide cooler and ice. You save money versus bar prices.
What's the group size limit?
Boat fits up to 12 people. Bachelorette groups, birthday crews, friend groups—all welcome. Larger groups? We have multiple boats available.
Can we stop at bars?
Yes. We can dock at waterfront bars and restaurants. Hit multiple spots, grab food, party on the boat, then dock again. Your call.
