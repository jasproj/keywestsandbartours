Frequently Asked Questions

What experience level is needed?
None! Beginners to experts welcome. Our captain teaches techniques and handles the hard work. You just relax and fish.
Do I need a fishing license?
No. It's included with your charter. We handle all paperwork. Just show up and fish.
Can we keep our catch?
Absolutely! We clean and fillet your fish. You can take it to your hotel or restaurant to cook. Many places will prepare it for dinner.
What fish will we catch?
Depends on season. Typical catches: snapper, grouper, permit, tarpon, kingfish, wahoo, mahi-mahi. Captain will target based on season and your preference.
